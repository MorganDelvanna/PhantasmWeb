Covid Restrictions

This is a list of everything we are doing to keep safe:

  • All attendees are to be fully vaccinated.
  • Masks will be warn when not eating or dinking.
  • Hand sanitizer and masks will be available at the main desk.
  • Maximum occupancy at tables will be reduced from 6 to 5.
  • We are going to double the number of outdoor tables available.
  • Game sign up will be done in an orderly fashion.
  • We will be increasing the number of gold coins given out, allowing guests to avoid lines and pre-sign up for games.
  • Our attendance numbers will be reduced to 25% in accordance current health regulations (that doesn’t effect us as much as it sounds as we tend to be more spread out than say a dinner event). For us it means we’ll be operating at about 75% capacity.
