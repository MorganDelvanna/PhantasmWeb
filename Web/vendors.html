Vendor Information

This year Phantasm is offering vendor space to prospective vendors. If you are interested in showing your wares at Phantasm contact us and let us know that you would like to sell & what your space requirements are. We will strive to accommodate all prospective vendors but our limited space is available on a first come basis.

Tables are rented for $50 for the weekend. There is a special for an ad space and two tables for two days.

Advertising

Space is available in the Phantasm convention booklet and on the web page at a very competitive rate. Every other page in the convention booklet is offers two 4 x 5 inch black & white spaces. Phantasm actively encourages ad swap programs. We are always looking for industry support and the donation of prizes. For further information / details please click here to email me.
