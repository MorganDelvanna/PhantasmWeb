Directions and Accommodations

Once you have reached Peterborough getting to the downtown is really quite simple. The following map illustrates the route. Take the 'Bensfort Road' exit off of Highway 115, turn left onto Bensfort Road, turn Right onto River Rd. S, turn left onto Lansdowne Street, turn right on George Street. The Holiday Inn will be on the right free on Saturday and Sundays.

All reservations must be made by March 13th, 2018. Any guestroom cancellations need to be done 24 hours prior to arrival.
